Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-21

Bravener Consulting (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or use our services. This Policy is designed to meet the requirements of the EU/UK GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the CPRA.

This document is provided for general information and is not legal advice. Your specific obligations may vary. Consult your counsel to finalize.

1) Scope

This Policy covers personal information processed by us as a controller when you browse this website, submit forms or emails, or engage us for managed services and projects.

2) What we collect

  • Identifiers: name, business email, phone, company.
  • Device/usage data: IP address, browser type, pages viewed.
  • Commercial & service data: records of services, proposals, communications.
  • Sensitive data: not intentionally collected via this site; managed via contract/BAA if applicable.

3) Sources

Directly from you, automatically from your device, and from service providers supporting our operations.

4) Purposes of use

  • Provide and improve services
  • Respond to inquiries and perform contracts
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • With consent: send updates you request

5) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Contract
  • Legitimate interests
  • Consent
  • Legal obligation

6) Sharing & disclosures

Service providers under contract; legal requirements; business transfers. We do not sell personal information or share it for cross‑context behavioral advertising.

7) Data retention

Kept only as long as needed, then deleted or anonymized.

8) Your rights

GDPR: access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, withdraw consent. California: know/access, delete, correct, opt‑out of sale/share (not applicable), limit sensitive PI (not applicable). No discrimination.

9) Exercising rights & verification

Email Sean@bravenerconsulting.com with subject “Privacy Request”. We may verify identity; authorized agents may submit with proof.

10) Cookies & analytics

Essential cookies and privacy‑respecting analytics with IP masking where feasible.

11) Security

Administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the data.

12) International transfers
