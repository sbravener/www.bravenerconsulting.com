Why build with Bravener

  • Performance tuned: sensible PBO/undervolt, RAM XMP/EXPO validation, and fan curves balanced for acoustics.
  • Reliability first: 48‑hour burn‑in, firmware updates, cable management, and thermals verified under load.
  • Simulator ready: triple‑monitor + VR planning, USB power & hub strategy for wheels, pedals, HOTAS, and button boxes.
