Design & Development

We start with a walkthrough and a quick inventory. Then we turn your “wish list” into clear user stories and automations. Typical deliverables include a device map, network plan, controller selection (e.g., Home Assistant Yellow/Blue, Raspberry Pi, NUC), and an automation spec with scenes and fail‑safes.

Deployment

  • Network-first: controller placement, VLANs for IoT, reliable Wi‑Fi
  • Controller build: backups, add‑ons, integrations, secure access
  • Device onboarding: consistent naming, rooms, and groups
  • Automation rollout: scenes, routines, presence, and notifications
  • Validation: interference checks, power budgets, firmware policy

Troubleshooting & Rescue

We fix flaky automations, bridge failures, “device unavailable”, and slow hubs. We’ll trace RF issues (Zigbee channel overlap, 2.4GHz congestion), repair broken mesh, and recover controllers from bad updates — without nuking your setup.
